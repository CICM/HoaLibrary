The associated Legendre polynomials. 

The function computes the associated Legendre polynomial $P(l, m)$ that is a part of the formula that compute the spherical harmonic coefficient where l is the band and the m is the argument of a spherical harmonic and x is the cosinus of the elevation. It uses three recurrence formulas \-: \[P(l, l)(x) = (-1)^l \times (2l - 1)!! \times (1 - x^2)^{0.5l}\] \[P(l + 1, l)(x) = x \times (2l + 1) \times P(l, l)\] \[P(l + 1, m)(x) = \frac{(2l + 1) \times x \times P(m, l) - (l + m) \times P(m, l - 1)}{(l - m + 1)}\] with $0 \leq l$ and $-l \leq m \leq +l$
