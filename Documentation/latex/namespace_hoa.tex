The associated Legendre polynomials. 

\begin{DoxyVerb}The function computes the associated Legendre polynomial \form#7 that is a part of the formula that compute the spherical harmonic coefficient where l is the band and the m is the argument of a spherical harmonic and x is the cosinus of the elevation. \end{DoxyVerb}
